IELTS Speaking Tips

The IELTS Speaking test assesses your spoken English skills in three parts and takes 11–14 minutes. It's a face-to-face interview with a certified examiner.

Test Format

  • Part 1 (Introduction): Answer questions about yourself and your life.
  • Part 2 (Long Turn): Talk for 1–2 minutes on a topic given on a cue card.
  • Part 3 (Discussion): Answer more abstract questions related to the Part 2 topic.

Tips to Improve

  • Speak clearly and naturally. Don’t memorize answers.
  • Expand your answers with examples and explanations.
  • Use a range of vocabulary and grammatical structures.
  • Practice common topics with a partner or record yourself.

Practice Topics

  • Hometown, education, hobbies, work
  • Describe a favorite book/movie
  • Technology, travel, environment, health
