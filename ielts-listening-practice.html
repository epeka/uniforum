IELTS Listening Practice

The IELTS Listening test is designed to assess your ability to understand spoken English in academic and everyday settings. It consists of four parts with a total of 40 questions.

Test Format

  • Part 1: Conversation in a social context (e.g., booking a hotel room)
  • Part 2: A monologue in a social context (e.g., a museum guide)
  • Part 3: Conversation in an academic context (e.g., a discussion between students and a professor)
  • Part 4: A lecture or talk on an academic subject

Tips for Success

  • Practice listening to a range of accents: British, Australian, Canadian, etc.
  • Focus on the keywords in questions before listening.
  • Learn to predict information and follow the speakerâ€™s train of thought.
  • Be careful with spelling and grammar when writing your answers.
