IELTS Writing Task 2

Task 2 of the IELTS Writing exam asks you to write an essay in response to a point of view, argument, or problem. This task contributes twice as much to your writing band score compared to Task 1.

Essay Types

  • Opinion (Agree or Disagree)
  • Discussion (Discuss Both Views)
  • Problem/Solution
  • Advantages/Disadvantages
  • Double Question

What You Need to Do

  • Write at least 250 words in about 40 minutes.
  • Support your arguments with clear examples and explanations.
  • Organize your ideas logically with proper paragraphing.
  • Use a formal, academic tone.

Tips for a High Score

  • Understand the question type and structure your response accordingly.
  • Use a range of vocabulary and grammatical structures.
  • Avoid repeating words and ideas.
  • Edit your essay for clarity and errors before submitting.

Example Task

Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programs. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Sample Introduction: "It is argued that high school students should be required to engage in unpaid community service. This essay agrees that volunteering can help students develop valuable skills and contribute positively to society."
