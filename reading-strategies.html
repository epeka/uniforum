Reading Strategies

The SAT Reading section tests your ability to comprehend and analyze written passages. Here are some effective strategies to improve your score:

  • Skim the passage: Get a general idea of the passage structure and main points before answering questions.
  • Focus on keywords: Look for keywords in questions and locate them in the passage to find answers efficiently.
  • Understand question types: Familiarize yourself with questions on main ideas, details, vocabulary in context, and inference.
  • Eliminate wrong answers: Use the process of elimination to narrow down answer choices.
  • Practice active reading: Annotate passages and summarize paragraphs mentally to stay engaged.

Regular practice with real SAT reading passages will build your speed and accuracy.
