Writing & Language Tips

The SAT Writing & Language section assesses grammar, punctuation, and effective language use. Follow these tips:

  • Master grammar rules: Focus on subject-verb agreement, verb tenses, pronoun clarity, and parallelism.
  • Improve sentence structure: Avoid run-ons and fragments; practice combining sentences smoothly.
  • Use concise language: Eliminate redundant words and phrases.
  • Pay attention to punctuation: Correctly use commas, semicolons, colons, and apostrophes.
  • Practice editing passages: Take practice tests and carefully review explanations for errors.

Regular writing and revision will improve your ability to spot and correct mistakes quickly.
