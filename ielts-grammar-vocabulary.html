IELTS Grammar & Vocabulary

Grammar and vocabulary are crucial for achieving a high band score in both IELTS Writing and Speaking. Examiners assess your accuracy and range of language.

Key Grammar Skills

  • Complex sentence structures (e.g., conditionals, relative clauses)
  • Correct use of tenses
  • Passive voice and modal verbs
  • Subject-verb agreement
  • Articles, prepositions, and punctuation

Vocabulary Tips

  • Use topic-specific vocabulary for common IELTS topics (e.g., environment, education, health)

  • Include collocations and idiomatic expressions where appropriate
  • Learn academic phrases for Writing Task 2
