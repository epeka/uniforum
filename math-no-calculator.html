Math - No Calculator

The Math No Calculator section tests your fundamental math skills without the aid of a calculator. Key tips include:

  • Practice mental math: Improve your ability to do calculations quickly in your head.
  • Master algebraic manipulation: Be comfortable rearranging equations and simplifying expressions.
  • Know geometry formulas: Have area, perimeter, volume, and basic trigonometry formulas memorized.
  • Understand number properties: Practice working with factors, multiples, primes, and divisibility rules.
  • Use estimation: When stuck, approximate values to eliminate wrong answer choices.

Consistent practice of no-calculator problems will boost your confidence and accuracy.
