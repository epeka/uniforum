IELTS Writing Task 1

Writing Task 1 of the IELTS Academic test asks you to describe visual information such as charts, graphs, diagrams, and maps. You should summarize the main features and make comparisons where relevant.

What You Need to Do

  • Write a minimum of 150 words in about 20 minutes.
  • Identify and describe main trends, differences, and stages in processes.
  • Use formal and objective language.

Types of Questions

  • Bar Charts
  • Line Graphs
  • Pie Charts
  • Tables
  • Maps
  • Process Diagrams

Tips for a High Score

  • Group similar information logically.
  • Use a variety of sentence structures and vocabulary.
  • Avoid giving opinions or explanations â€” just describe the data.
  • Practice paraphrasing the task question effectively.

Example Task

The graph below shows the number of international students who enrolled in a UK university from 2010 to 2020.

Sample Introduction: "The line graph compares the number of overseas students who registered at a UK university over a ten-year period from 2010 to 2020."
